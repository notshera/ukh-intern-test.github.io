Form 1 – Expectation Essay

Write a comprehensive essay (500–750 words) discussing your expectations for your internship experience. Focus on the following points:

  • Learning objectives: What specific skills or knowledge do you hope to gain?
  • Professional development: How will this internship contribute to your career goals? How do you plan to add value to your internship site?
  • Work environment: What are your expectations regarding the workplace culture and dynamics?
  • Challenges: Anticipate any potential challenges you may face and how you plan to address them.
